Our story

In business for over thirty years, our establishment remains true to its original mission; treat the customers as you would treat your family. Mandarette Chinese Cafe is a spin off from the Mandarin of Beverly Hills in 1984. In 1989, head chef, Yang took over the operation and today he is still the head chef. Over the years we have proven to be one of the best chinese restaurants in the area!
